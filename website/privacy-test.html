Auraly Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

🔒 100% Privacy Guarantee

Auraly does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

📱 Local Storage Only

All reminders, settings, and recordings are stored locally on your Mac. Your data never leaves your device.

🚫 No Tracking

The app does not use analytics, ads, or any tracking technologies.

🤝 No Third Parties

No user data is shared with third parties.

🔐 System Permissions

Auraly may request local permissions only to provide its core features:

  • Microphone: For voice recording reminders
  • Notifications: To alert you when reminders are due
  • Accessibility: For global hotkeys (⌘⇧R)

These permissions are handled entirely by macOS and can be revoked at any time via System Settings.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@auraly.app
